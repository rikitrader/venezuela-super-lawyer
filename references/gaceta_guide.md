# Gaceta Oficial Citation Guide

## Official Publication Standards for Venezuelan Legal Practice

---

## What is the Gaceta Oficial?

The Gaceta Oficial de la República Bolivariana de Venezuela is the official publication where all Venezuelan laws, decrees, regulations, and other normative acts must be published to have legal effect.

### Legal Basis
- Ley de Publicaciones Oficiales
- Art. 215 CRBV (presidential promulgation)
- General principle: No norm is effective until published in Gaceta Oficial

---

## Types of Gaceta Oficial

### 1. Gaceta Oficial Ordinaria
- Regular publication
- Most common type
- Sequential numbering

### 2. Gaceta Oficial Extraordinaria
- Special publication
- For urgent or important matters
- Separate sequential numbering
- Published outside regular schedule

---

## Citation Format

### Standard Format
```
[Nombre de la norma], Gaceta Oficial [Ordinaria/Extraordinaria] No. [número], [fecha completa].
```

### Examples

#### Constitution
```
Constitución de la República Bolivariana de Venezuela, Gaceta Oficial Extraordinaria No. 36.860, 30 de diciembre de 1999.
```

#### Organic Law
```
Ley Orgánica de Hidrocarburos, Gaceta Oficial Ordinaria No. 38.493, 04 de agosto de 2006.
```

#### Decree
```
Decreto No. 2.165, Gaceta Oficial Ordinaria No. 41.452, 24 de julio de 2018.
```

#### Resolution
```
Resolución No. 001-2023 del Ministerio del Poder Popular para Relaciones Interiores, Gaceta Oficial Ordinaria No. 42.789, 15 de marzo de 2023.
```

---

## Essential Elements

### Required Components
1. **Name of the norm** - Complete official title
2. **Type of Gaceta** - Ordinaria or Extraordinaria
3. **Number** - Gaceta number (not the norm's internal number)
4. **Date** - Full date of publication

### Optional but Recommended
- Internal norm number (Decree No., Resolution No., etc.)
- Issuing authority
- Date of enactment (if different from publication)

---

## Verification Requirements

### For Every Cited Norm, Verify:

1. **Original Publication**
   - Gaceta number
   - Publication date
   - Type (Ordinaria/Extraordinaria)

2. **Reforms (if any)**
   - Each reform's Gaceta number
   - Reform dates
   - Scope of changes (total/partial)

3. **Current Status**
   - Vigente (In force)
   - Derogada (Repealed)
   - Modificada (Modified)
   - Suspendida (Suspended)

4. **Transitional Provisions**
   - Entry into force date
   - Transitional regimes
   - Vacatio legis periods

## Common Errors to Avoid

### 1. Citing Without Gaceta Reference
❌ "La Ley Orgánica de Hidrocarburos establece..."
✅ "La Ley Orgánica de Hidrocarburos (G.O. No. 38.493, 04-08-2006) establece..."

### 2. Confusing Norm Number with Gaceta Number
❌ "Decreto 2.165, Gaceta Oficial No. 2.165"
✅ "Decreto No. 2.165, Gaceta Oficial No. 41.452"

### 3. Citing Repealed Norms as Current
- Always verify current status before citing
- If citing historical context, indicate the norm is no longer in force

### 4. Missing Type Designation
❌ "Gaceta Oficial No. 36.860"
✅ "Gaceta Oficial Extraordinaria No. 36.860"

## Citation Templates

### For Legal Documents

#### In Pleadings
```
De conformidad con lo establecido en el artículo [X] de la [Nombre de la Ley]
(Gaceta Oficial [Ordinaria/Extraordinaria] No. [número], [fecha])...
```

#### In Legal Opinions
```
La [Nombre de la Norma], publicada en Gaceta Oficial [Tipo] No. [número]
de fecha [fecha], establece en su artículo [X] que...
```

#### In Footnotes
```
[Nombre de la Norma], G.O. [Ord./Ext.] No. [número], [fecha], art. [X].
```

### Abbreviated Format (After First Full Citation)
```
First: Ley Orgánica de Hidrocarburos, Gaceta Oficial Ordinaria No. 38.493,
       04 de agosto de 2006 (en adelante "LOH").
After: LOH, art. [X].
```

## Validity Verification Checklist

Before citing any norm:

```
□ Located original Gaceta publication
□ Verified Gaceta type (Ordinaria/Extraordinaria)
□ Confirmed Gaceta number
□ Confirmed publication date
□ Searched for subsequent reforms
□ Verified current status (vigente/derogada)
□ Checked for transitional provisions
□ Noted any pending reforms or challenges
```

## Special Situations

### Republications
Some norms are republished due to errors:
```
[Nombre], Gaceta Oficial [Tipo] No. [número], [fecha].
Reimpresa por error material en G.O. No. [número], [fecha].
```

### Partial Repeals
```
[Nombre], G.O. No. [número], [fecha].
Artículos [X-Y] derogados por [Ley Derogatoria], G.O. No. [número], [fecha].
```

### Constitutional Challenges
If a norm has been challenged before TSJ:
```
[Nombre], G.O. No. [número], [fecha].
Nota: Artículo [X] declarado inconstitucional por TSJ/SC, Sent. No. [X], [fecha].
```
